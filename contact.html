How can I place an order? +

You can place an order by contacting us via phone (0836982296), email (Dressmeclothing@yahoo.com), or by using the chat assistant on our website. We'll guide you through the ordering process.

Do you offer international shipping? +

Yes! We ship to over 15 countries worldwide. Shipping costs and delivery times vary by location. Contact us for a quote based on your destination.

Absolutely! We specialize in bespoke tailoring. Share your ideas, preferred fabrics, and measurements, and our design team will create a one-of-a-kind piece just for you.
